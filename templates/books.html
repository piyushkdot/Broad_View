Heaven By Mieko Kawakami


"Heaven" by Mieko Kawakami is a poignant novel about two bullied teenagers who form a deep, emotional bond, exploring themes of friendship, suffering, and the struggle for meaning amidst cruelty, highlighting human resilience and the search for connection.

Out By Natsuo Kirino


"Out" by Natsuo Kirino is a gripping thriller about four women factory workers in Tokyo who become entangled in crime and violence after covering up a murder, delving into themes of desperation, survival, and the dark side of human nature.


Buy From Here :


Before The Coffee Gets Cold by Toshikazu Kawaguchi


"Before the Coffee Gets Cold" by Toshikazu Kawaguchi is a heartwarming tale set in a Tokyo café where customers can travel back in time, exploring themes of love, regret, and hope as they seize the chance to change moments from their past.


Buy From Here :


The Silent Patient by Alex Michaelides


"The Silent Patient" by Alex Michaelides is a psychological thriller about a famous painter who stops speaking after allegedly murdering her husband, and the psychotherapist determined to uncover the truth behind her silence.


Buy From Here :


Then She Was Gone By Lisa Jwell


"Then She Was Gone" by Lisa Jewell is a gripping mystery about a mother who, ten years after her teenage daughter’s disappearance, begins to uncover shocking truths when she meets a young girl eerily resembling her missing child.
